About Us

Know More About Us

01. History

Innovis Construction was established on September 9, 2008 by John Ferrer Baguilod, a former Project Manager at Cemex - Thailand. Currently, the main office is located nearby Solid Cement Corporation in Antipolo, Rizal. It is a company that provides construction, renovation and maintenance services.

02. Mission

The company aims to provide quality and on time delivery of services that are engineered to last while considering the safety of its employees in the operation of project execution.

03. Vision

Innovis Construction envisions the company to be the leading construction and maintenance service provider and supplier of industrial equipment and parts in the Philippines.
